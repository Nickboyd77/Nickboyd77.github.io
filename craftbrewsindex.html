Seattle Craft Beers

Seattle's craft brewing industry has exploded in recent years, going from a few breweries to a rich and diverse beer scene. Across the city, there are small communities of microbreweries brewing beers in their own styles and offering tasting rooms for enthusiasts to try and enjoy their beers.
